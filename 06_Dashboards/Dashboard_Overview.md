## **Dashboard Overview**

This project contains **8 Power BI dashboards** designed to provide executive visibility, operational control, revenue protection,
capacity planning, and AI-driven actioning for healthcare operations.

All dashboards are built on a unified data model and are aligned with business objectives, RCA findings, and KPIs defined in the BRD, FRD, and SRS.

---

## **1. Executive Performance Overview**
**Audience:** CEO, COO  
**Purpose:** Enterprise-level health check of operations and revenue

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Appointments
- No-Show Percentage
- Average Patient Wait Time
- Total Revenue
- Doctor Utilization Percentage

**Key Insights:**
- Monthly appointment volume trends
- Revenue contribution by department
- Appointment status distribution (Completed / No-Show / Cancelled)

---

## **2. Revenue Leakage & Recovery**
**Audience:** Finance Head, Leadership  
**Purpose:** Identify revenue loss and recovery potential

**Key KPIs:**
- Revenue Leakage %
- Revenue Lost due to No-Shows
- Recoverable Revenue (Medium Risk)

**Key Insights:**
- No-show trend as a proxy for revenue leakage
- Department-wise revenue contribution
- Potential revenue recovery opportunities

---

## **3. Appointment Flow & Bottlenecks**
**Audience:** Operations Head  
**Purpose:** Detect operational bottlenecks impacting patient experience

**Key Insights:**
- Department-level wait time bottlenecks
- No-show rate by slot type
- Average wait time by slot
- Doctor utilization vs patient wait time correlation

---

## **4. Doctor Utilization & Capacity Planning**
**Audience:** Medical Director, Operations  
**Purpose:** Optimize doctor workload and capacity

**Key Insights:**
- Doctor utilization % by individual doctor
- Doctor load vs daily capacity
- Utilization vs no-show relationship
- Utilization comparison by employment type (Full-Time vs Visiting)

---

## **5. AI No-Show Prediction & Actioning**
**Audience:** Leadership, Operations  
**Purpose:** Enable proactive intervention using AI predictions

**Key Insights:**
- Elevated-risk appointments (Prediction > threshold)
- Prediction confidence distribution
- Risk bucket distribution (Low / Medium)
- Actual vs predicted no-show outcomes

---

## **6. Patient Behavior & Risk Segmentation**
**Audience:** Operations, CX Teams  
**Purpose:** Understand patient behavior patterns

**Key Insights:**
- No-show rate trend across age groups
- Patient volume by age group
- No-show rate comparison by age segment

---

## **7. Agile Delivery & Adoption Summary**
**Audience:** Leadership  
**Purpose:** Demonstrate analytics delivery impact using Agile methodology

**Key Insights:**
- Sprints completed
- Dashboards delivered
- Stakeholders impacted
- Sprint-wise dashboard delivery mapping
- Sprint-wise reduction in no-show rate

---

## **8. Management Action & Business Impact**
**Audience:** CEO, COO  
**Purpose:** Drive decision-making and ownership

**Key Insights:**
- Appointments requiring intervention
- Revenue at risk
- Primary operational bottlenecks
- Expected business impact (next 90 days)
- Action items with owners and expected outcomes
