## **Business Problem Statement**

### **Background**
A multi-hospital healthcare organization was experiencing increasing operational inefficiencies 
despite having access to large volumes of appointment and clinical data.

Leadership lacked a consolidated, data-driven view of operational performance across hospitals, departments, and doctors.

---

### **Key Business Challenges**
- Rising **appointment no-show rates**, impacting revenue and utilization
- **Under-utilization of doctors** in certain departments and time slots
- **Revenue leakage** due to missed appointments and inefficient scheduling
- Absence of **predictive insights** to proactively manage patient behavior

---

### **Business Need**
Leadership required an **analytics-driven solution** that could:
- Provide a consolidated operational view
- Identify patterns and inefficiencies
- Predict high-risk no-show appointments
- Enable proactive, data-backed decision-making

---

### **Success Criteria**
- Improved visibility into appointment and utilization metrics
- Reduction in no-show rates
- Better clinical capacity planning
- Actionable insights for leadership and operations teams
