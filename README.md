##MediCore Health — Healthcare Analytics & Predictive BI Project##

BUSINESS CONTEXT
This engagement reflects real-world healthcare analytics work carried out for a multi-hospital healthcare organization operating at scale.
Senior leadership was facing persistent challenges such as:
High appointment no-show rates
Revenue leakage across departments
Inefficient doctor utilization
Limited visibility into future operational risks
The objective was to shift from reactive reporting to predictive, insight-driven decision-making.

PROJECT OBJECTIVES
Reduce appointment no-show rates using predictive insights
Improve doctor utilization and capacity planning
Identify revenue recovery opportunities
Enable leadership with clear, actionable analytics

MY ROLE — ANALYTICS CONSULTANT
I worked closely with business and leadership stakeholders to deliver this engagement end-to-end.
Key responsibilities included:
Translating leadership concerns into measurable KPIs
Defining business and functional requirements (BRD, FRD, SRS)
Designing and validating data models using SQL
Building executive-ready Power BI dashboards
Developing a predictive model to identify high-risk no-show appointments
Converting analytical insights into practical business actions

TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES
SQL — Data modeling, validation, and business queries
Python — Predictive analytics and risk scoring
Power BI — Executive and operational dashboards
Business Analysis — BRD, FRD, SRS, KPI definition
Agile Delivery — Stakeholder collaboration and iterative delivery

DASHBOARD COVERAGE
Executive Healthcare Overview
Appointment & No-Show Analysis
Revenue & Billing Insights
Doctor Utilization & Capacity Planning
Patient Risk Segmentation
AI-Based No-Show Prediction
Management Action Summary
Each dashboard was designed to answer specific leadership questions, not just present metrics.

PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS
A machine-learning model was developed to predict the probability of appointment no-shows using historical appointment behavior and operational factors.
Risk outputs enabled:
Targeted patient follow-ups
Proactive schedule optimization
Improved clinical capacity utilization

BUSINESS IMPACT
Identified potential 12–18% reduction in no-shows
Highlighted under-utilized clinical capacity
Exposed revenue leakage patterns
Enabled leadership to take proactive, data-driven decisions
